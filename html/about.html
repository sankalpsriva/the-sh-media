About Us
We are a team of students with a passion for anything and everything related to media. What started as BGSH, a Barstool styled page for Beech Grove High School, has grown into a full-fledged brand. Now we’re doing it even bigger at the University of Indianapolis with UIndySH, a page that has grown three times as large, with over 1 million views across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube in just 70 days. Along the way, we realized we’re not just good at creating moments, but capturing them too. What began with sports photography has expanded into all types of photography and videography, from events to custom productions.
